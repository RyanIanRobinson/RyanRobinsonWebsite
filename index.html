About Me

Hi, I'm Ryan Robinson!

If I had to pick one word to sum myself up, it would probably be “quirky” (or maybe “weird” if we're being honest). I'm someone who wakes up at 0200 on Saturdays to catch the astronomical, nautical, and civil dawn—yes, military time is a permanent setting in my life. I’ll also challenge anyone to a mental arithmetic duel any day of the week.

When I'm not pondering life's mysteries or climbing mountains, you can find me creating weird and wonderful gifts for my friends. Who doesn’t love a surprise matching shirt set or a 2-meter teddy bear? I'm deeply passionate about math and science, and now I'm diving into trading, astronomy, and health sciences through my studies, reading, and coding.

Welcome to my slice of the internet, where data, humor, and a bit of weirdness collide!
